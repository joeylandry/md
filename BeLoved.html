BeLoved for Teens

Rooted in the Eucharist and the heart of Jesus, BeLoved for Teens invites young people into prayer, conversation, and community.

Interactive talks, small groups, and time before the Blessed Sacrament help them discover who they are in Christ.

BeLoved for Adults

A retreat space for adults to rest, encounter mercy, and be renewed in the sacraments.

Talks, guided reflection, and shared prayer help participants carry the peace of Christ back into their homes and parishes.
